Ferrari
Ferrari SpA is an Italian car manufacturer based in Maranello , Italy . Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1947 , the brand is considered one of the best automotive brands entire history and probably the best known brand in the world 5 . As a manufacturer of prestige sports cars, Ferrari has quickly established itself as an automotive reference, both technically and aesthetically. The history of Ferrari is inseparable from that of Scuderia Ferrari , a racing team evolving in Sport Prototypes as well as in Grand Touring - and later in Formula 1 - since 1929 , with which the manufacturer has had its greatest successes. With its experience in competition , the brand "horse prancing" ( " crawling cavallino " ) draws from the techniques on its series models, as evidenced by Ferrari 288 GTO , F50 or Enzo , models with exceptional performance. From the 166MM , Enzo Anselmo Ferrari's first automobile bearing his name, to the most recent, the Ferrari 488 Pista , Ferrari still arouses an "irresistible fascination" 6 . Enzo Ferrari also liked to describe a Ferrari automobile as "the incarnation of a beautiful mechanic for men who have the desire to reward themselves, to realize a dream and to breathe for a long time into their lives the fire of youthful passion "
Bentley
he Bentley Boys were a group of enthusiastic British motorists including Woolf Barnato , Sir Henry "Tim" Birkin , steeplechase chaser George Duller, aviator Glen Kidston , automotive journalist SCH "Sammy" Davis , and Dr. Dudley Benjafield . The Bentley Boys favored Bentley cars. Many were fortunate and often had a military background. They kept alive the brand's reputation for high performance; Bentley was noted for his four consecutive wins at the 24 Hours of Le Mans from 1927 to 1930. In 1929, Tim Birkin developed the lightweight 4½-liter Blower Bentley at Welwyn Garden City and produced five racing specials, starting with the Bentley Blower No.1 which was optimized for the Brooklands Circuit . Birkin put the model on the market before it was fully developed. As a result he was not reliable.
Lamborgini
Lamborghini is a car manufacturer founded in 1951 by the industrialist Ferruccio Lamborghini and installed in Sant'Agata Bolognese in Italy . The company initially specialized in the construction of agricultural tractors designed to meet a growing demand for Italy devastated by the war and in reconstruction. Soon, she became the third industrial tractor manufacturer in Italy , behind Fiat and Ferguson . Having made a fortune in just ten years, Ferruccio Lamborghini decided to satisfy his passion for "beautiful mechanics" and Italian GT . Eager to produce more efficient, more sophisticated and more reliable cars than Ferrari and Maserati , he founded the October 30 , 1963, the company Automobili Lamborghini specializes in the production of prestige sports cars . In 1971 , the agricultural equipment company Trattori Lamborghini was sold to the group Same Deutz-Fahr Group , Lamborghini devoting itself then only to the design of automobiles. Many companies will acquire the Italian firm until 1998, when Lamborghini is taken over by the German Audi ( Volkswagen Group ), the current owner.
